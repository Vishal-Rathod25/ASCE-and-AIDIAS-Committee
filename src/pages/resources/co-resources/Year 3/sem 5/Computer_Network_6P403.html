Subject Description

Computer networks is an applied level course aims to use and share resources over the network for global connectivity. This course obtains requisite knowledge about hardware and software tools and requirements of networks to acquire skills for establishment of network. Students will learn to maintain and troubleshoot computer networks with its resources and services.
